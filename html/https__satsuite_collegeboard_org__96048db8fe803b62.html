    In-School Educator Experience

    We want to make sure you and your staff feel as prepared as possible ahead of fall 2024 testing. The Educator Experience includes everything you need to know leading up to test day.

    Enter the In-School Educator Experience

    In-School Testing Dates

    Fall 2024

    TEST TESTING DATES
    PSAT 8/9 Oct. 1–31
    PSAT/NMSQT Oct. 1–31
    SAT School Day Oct. 1–31
    • Districts and schools will be able to administer the PSAT/NMSQT, PSAT 8/9, and SAT School Day on as many days of the testing window as needed.
    • For fall 2024 only, the PSAT/NMSQT will be offered on two Saturdays (October 12 and October 26) to accommodate schools or students who cannot test on October 12 due to Yom Kippur.
    • See Test Dates and Deadlines for ordering and registration deadlines for the fall administrations will be available in the spring.

    Spring 2025

    TEST TESTING DATES
    PSAT 8/9 March 3–April 30
    PSAT 10 March 3–April 30
    SAT School Day March 3–April 30
    • Ordering and registration deadlines for the spring administrations will be available in the fall.
    • Districts and schools will be able to administer all three assessments on as many days of the testing window as needed. If your state requires you to administer SAT School Day, PSAT 10 or PSAT 8/9, your state may determine your testing schedule.

    Bluebook Technology

    Bluebook™ is a testing app that makes test day easier and faster for students and proctors. Bluebook supports testing on Mac and Windows devices, iPads, and school-managed Chromebooks. Explore the Bluebook app and get more information about technical specifications and downloading to managed devices.

    Discover Bluebook

    District/School Pricing

    College Board is introducing new price adjustments for schools and districts that serve low-income students and those that administer assessments in both the fall and the spring.

    Learn About Pricing

    Introducing BigFuture School

    BigFuture® School is a mobile app designed to spark students' interest in planning for the future. Available to students who take certain College Board assessments during the school day, it provides college and career guidance the moment they get their scores.

    Explore BigFuture School

    Digital SAT Weekend

    Transitioning to Digital

    The digital SAT Suite of Assessments will continue to measure the knowledge and skills students are learning in high school that matter most for college and career readiness. The changes were informed by extensive conversations with school and district leaders. Through the transition resources will be provided to ensure school and district staff feel confident in the move to digital. Explore details on test structure, scoring, and content for the digital SAT Suite of Assessments.
